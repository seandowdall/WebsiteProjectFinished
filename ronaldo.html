Who is Cristiano Ronaldo?

Cristiano Ronaldo is a Portuguese professional footballer currently playing for Serie A club Juventus. He also captains the Portugal national team. Ronaldo was born 5 February 1985 and ever since a child dreamt of becoming one of the best players in the world. He was born and raised in Madeira and began his senior club career at Sporting CP. Ronaldo then signed for Manchester United at the age of 18, which would prove to be a life changing move. Achievements:

Ronaldo has won 5 Ballon d'Or awards and has won a total of 31 major trophies in his career thus far. He also has 4 European Golden Boots.
